abstract: >-
  This paper explores using cryptographic accumulators as a secure, privacy-preserving method for managing the lifecycle of digital identity credentials. By analyzing performance both analytically and experimentally, the research demonstrates that these accumulators—combined with zero-knowledge proofs—offer a viable and efficient alternative to traditional certificate revocation schemes.
